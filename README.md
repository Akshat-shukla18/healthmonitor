# healthmonitor
🩺 HealthMonitor

HealthMonitor is a web-based platform that allows users to upload and analyze their medical reports, providing intelligent insights and personalized health suggestions.

🚀Features
📄 Report Analysis
Upload your medical reports and get a structured breakdown of key metrics.
~ Health Insights
Understand whether your parameters are within normal ranges.
⚠️ Abnormality Detection
Highlights values that are outside recommended limits.
Personalized Suggestions
Get actionable recommendations based on your report.
User-Friendly Interface
Simple and intuitive UI for easy navigation.
